HawaMahal

HawaMahal, also known as the "Palace of Winds," is a famous architectural landmark in Jaipur, India, renowned for its intricate honeycomb-like facade with 953 small windows, designed to allow royal women to observe street life while remaining hidden from view.

Jaisalmer Fort

Jaisalmer Fort, often called the "Golden Fort," is a majestic sandstone fortress rising from the Thar Desert in Rajasthan, India, renowned for its intricate architecture and its role as a living fort, still inhabited by locals. It stands as a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a testament to the rich history of the region.

6:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Mount Abu

Mount Abu, Rajasthan's only hill station, is nestled in the lush Aravalli Range, known for its cool climate, serene lakes, and the stunning Dilwara Jain Temples, showcasing intricate marble architecture. It's a popular retreat in the desert state of Rajasthan, offering a refreshing contrast to the arid landscapes.

9:00 AM - 5:30 PM
book now

Ranthambore

Ranthambore National Park, situated in Rajasthan, India, is a renowned wildlife sanctuary and tiger reserve, famous for its diverse wildlife, including Bengal tigers, leopards, and various bird species. Its historic Ranthambore Fort adds a unique cultural element to the park's natural beauty.

6:30 AM - 10:00 PM
book now

Bikaner

Junagarh Fort, located in Bikaner, Rajasthan, is an architectural marvel renowned for its stunning blend of Rajput and Mughal styles, featuring intricate stone carvings and ornate palaces. This impregnable fortress stands as a testament to Bikaner's rich history and craftsmanship.

10:00 AM - 4:30 PM
